About OpenEdge

OpenEdge works with a government-funded outreach program through California Lifeline to provide smartphone devices to specific regions of low income communities. We target the people and communities who will most benefit from the wireless services we promote.

The Impact

The importance of technology in today’s world is paramount to the success and well-being of these communities. Not only can individuals apply for jobs, but California deems cell phone service to be a necessary part of life - and therefore subsidizes this program to allow for communication and connection to thrive.

By helping the disadvantaged work as positive benefactors to the community at large, we are working toward a better future.

Making communities stronger

We make communities stronger by providing these crucial services. Our coordinators work with various institutions to coordinate events in the communities at large. Our face to face basis for representation is the foundation for how we are able to build such trust with the community. By presenting the benefits of wireless service to low-income families, we bridge the gap between the underprivileged and their ability to become positive impacts in their communities.
